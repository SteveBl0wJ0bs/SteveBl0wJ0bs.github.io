In this section, we represent through a sankey diagram the flux between
the carbon storage and the ten most present tree categories
and between the ten most present tree categories and euro saved.
Move the cursor on the edges to see details.
Since the total carbon storage is too much greater
than the euro saved one, and due to the height of the svg
which denotes a limit to ensure the readability of the diagram, the height
of the carbon storage node and the euro saved node, and so the height
of the edges, is equal among them.
